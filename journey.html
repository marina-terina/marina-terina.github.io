Hi!
I'm Marina. Junior Web Developer
I come from a small town in northwest Belarus, always dreaming big and wanting to explore. My ambitions took me to Moscow, Russia, where I graduated from a top law school. I always wanted to travel, which is how I ended up in the aviation industry. I traveled the world and found my home in beautiful Australia. During the lockdown, when we were all stuck at home, I tried to entertain myself with online courses, many of which had suddenly become free. I discovered my passion for coding and wrote my first "Hello World."
That's how it all began...

Currently, I am a proud student of the She Codes Plus Brisbane program and a member of the wonderful She Codes Australia community. During the program, I will learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Django, and many more. I can't wait to fill out my skills section with more icons and my portfolio with more exciting projects. Each project is a unique opportunity to learn new skills and create something useful for the world.


Fun fact about me: I have visited over 40 countries.
Boring fact about me: I love classical music.
